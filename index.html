Privacy Policy for Ibanmatik

Effective Date: April 15, 2025

Ibanmatik is a simple utility app designed to help users quickly identify bank information based on IBAN numbers. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how your data is (or is not) collected and handled.

1. Data Collection

Ibanmatik does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). The app functions entirely on-device and does not require you to log in, sign up, or provide any personal data.

2. Payment Information

Ibanmatik does not collect or process any form of payment information such as credit card numbers, bank account details, or similar financial data. The app does not include any in-app purchases or payment processing features.

3. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external APIs that track or store user data.

4. Security

Since the app does not collect or transmit user data, there is no risk of data breach from our side.

5. Changes to This Policy
